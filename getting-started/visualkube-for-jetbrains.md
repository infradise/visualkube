# Plugin Location

You can find Visualkube for JetBrains IDEs on the official Marketplace:

https://plugins.jetbrains.com/plugin/28516-visualkube

## Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to get started with Visualkube in JetBrains IDEs:

<p>1. Install the plugin from the JetBrains <a href="https://plugins.jetbrains.com/plugin/28516-visualkube" target="_blank">Marketplace.</a></p>
<p>2. Click the Visualkube icon in the Tool Window, positioned at the top-left or top-center (refer to <a href="https://www.visualkube.com/updates/vendor/jetbrains/visualkube/screenshot" target="_blank">Understanding Plugin Placement in JetBrains IDEs</a> on our website).</p>
<p>3. Sign in with your `Visualkube` account.</p>
<p>4. Connect your Kubernetes(K8s) clusters from Local certificate slot space detected automatically</p>
<p>5. <b>(Optional)</b> Register your K8s cluster to Cloud certificate slot space for syncing among your devices. Now you can connect your K8s clusters anywhere, anytime.</p>
<p>6. Explore your K8s clusters or customize your K8s API.</p>
<p>7. Invite your team members or collaborators.</p>
<br />
<p>For more details, refer to the <a href="https://www.visualkube.com/docs/getting-started" target="_blank">Getting Started</a> or <a href="https://www.visualkube.com/docs/getting-started/quick-start" target="_blank">Quick Start</a> guides on our website. 
To install or uninstall the plugin, see the *IDE Integrations* section in the <a href="https://www.visualkube.com/docs/getting-started/installation" target="_blank">Installation Guide</a>
