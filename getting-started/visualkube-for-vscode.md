## Quick Setup Guide

Get started with Visualkube in VS Code or compatible IDEs by following these steps:

1. Install the plugin via the Marketplace or by using a VSIX file.

2. To run Visualkube, click the `Visualkube` icon in the Activation Bar (top-left), then select `â–¶ Visualkube: Run`. You can also press `F1` and choose `Visualkube: Run` from the Command Palette.

3. Sign in with your Visualkube account.

4. Connect your Kubernetes(K8s) clusters from Local certificate slot space detected automatically.

5. (Optional) Register your K8s cluster to Cloud certificate slot space for syncing among your devices. Now you can connect your K8s clusters anywhere, anytime.

6. Explore your K8s clusters or customize your K8s API.

7. Invite your team members or collaborators.

For more details, refer to the [*Getting Started*](https://www.visualkube.com/docs/getting-started) or [*Quick Start*](https://www.visualkube.com/docs/getting-started/quick-start) guides on our website. To install or uninstall the plugin, see the *IDE Integrations* section in the [Installation Guide](https://www.visualkube.com/docs/getting-started/installation).
